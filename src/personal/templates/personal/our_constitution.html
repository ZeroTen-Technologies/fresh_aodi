The activities of Africa of Our Dream is fully regulated by her constitution. The constitution is designed to ensure high accountability and transparency in the running of the foundation. The board of governors and trustees of Africa of Our Dream Initiative will continue to ensure that the aims and objectives of the foundation are fully actualized for sustained prosperity of the African people and the world at large.

We, therefore, employ all people of goodwill to support our initiatives, donate or join our Ambassadors membership as a volunteer or project facilitator.
