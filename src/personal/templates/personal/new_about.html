AODI Vision & Mission

To build a new Africa where individuals will thrive and have access to the necessary things of life by promoting, supporting and investing in health, education and opportunities as well as in human capital for sustained prosperity.

Aims and Objectives

Building a new Africa would mean creating a society where individuals will be able to thrive and at least have access to the basic things of life. The goals of AODI is based on the premise that if we invest in health, education and opportunities as well as in the human capital, invariably we lay the foundation for sustained prosperity. To this end, the aims and objectives of AODI is summarily

“Youth and women empowerment through education and capacity building.”
The foundation shall pursue this objective in four different ways as listed below bearing in mind the great quote by Joseph Addison.
Education is a companion which no misfortune can depress, no crime can destroy, no enemy can alienate, no despotism can enslave. At home, a friend, abroad, an introduction, in solitude a solace and in society an ornament. It chastens vice, it guides virtue, it gives at once grace and government to genius. Without it, what is man? A splendid slave, a reasoning savage.
- Joseph Addison

  • Women empowerment: The foundation shall run activities to empower the girl child and women in general while campaigning against gender-based violence and promoting gender balance, education for the girl child, equal opportunity for girls with their male counterparts.
  • Youth empowerment: The foundation shall run activities that aim to empower less privileged youths in the society. The action plan will include but not limited to skill workshops, training on skills related to information communication technology (ICT), entrepreneurial activities etc., all aimed at human capacity development.
  • Access to quality education: The foundation shall run activities that encourage children and adults to acquire sound education. Where possible, education materials, infrastructures, equipment, scholarships information, and engagements that would guarantee quality education for all will be carried out. Emphasis will be on Science Technology Engineering and Mathematics (STEM) education.
  • Access to quality Medicare: The foundation shall run activities that would help the less privileged access quality healthcare. Where possible, medicine and medical equipment can be provided among other things to ensure that people have access to quality health care irrespective of their status. Priority will be given to terminal as well as communicable diseases.

In the pursuit of our causes, AODI will always remember other great quotes relating to education by great men like kofi Annan, Nelson Mandela and Malcom X.

Knowledge is power. Information is liberating. Education is the premise of progress, in every society, in every family. - kofi Annan
Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world. - Nelson Mandela
Education is the passport to the future, for tomorrow belongs to those who prepare for it today. - Malcolm X

Foundation Constitution

The activities of Africa of Our Dream is fully regulated by her constitution. The constitution is designed to ensure high accountability and transparency in the running of the foundation. The board of governors and trustees of Africa of Our Dream Initiative will continue to ensure that the aims and objectives of the foundation are fully actualized for sustained prosperity of the African people and the world at large. We, therefore, employ all people of goodwill to support our initiatives, donate or join our Ambassadors membership as a volunteer or project facilitator. The constitution of the foundation can be accessed here.

AODI Membership

Africa of Our dream Initiative has two significant membership forms – Voting membership and non-voting membership. Only the foundation trustees are eligible for voting membership. They include the members of the governing boards. Non-voting memberships include the ambassadorial membership and the associate membership. The ambassadorial membership comprises the project facilitators and volunteers and is open to anyone who is committed to improving the lives of others through various humanitarian services.

  • Governing Board: The board of governors is the highest decision-making body of the foundation and incorporates all the principal officers of the foundation made up of the founding trustees
  • Ambassadorial membership
  • Associate membership
